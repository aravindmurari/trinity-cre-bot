# Trinity CRE — Services

Trinity CRE provides commercial real estate advisory for tenants, landlords, buyers, and sellers throughout Greater Atlanta across three service areas.

---

## 01. Industrial

**Subtitle:** Warehouse, distribution, and flex space across Greater Atlanta.

Atlanta's industrial market moves fast. Whether you're a business searching for the right space or an owner looking to lease your asset, Trinity CRE brings local expertise, real relationships, and data-driven strategy to every deal.

**For Tenants:**
- Market survey and property shortlisting
- Site tours and evaluation criteria
- LOI drafting and lease negotiation
- Timeline and move-in coordination

**For Landlords:**
- Pricing and positioning strategy
- Targeted tenant outreach and marketing
- Offer management and negotiation
- Lease execution support

**Ideal for:** Businesses seeking warehouse, distribution, manufacturing, or flex/R&D space; landlords looking to lease industrial assets in Greater Atlanta.

---

## 02. Office Space

**Subtitle:** Finding the right office environment for your team and your brand.

Office space decisions shape how your team works and how clients perceive your business. Trinity CRE represents both tenants seeking the right fit and owners maximizing occupancy — with no conflict of interest on either side.

**For Tenants:**
- Needs assessment and space planning
- Market survey and shortlisting
- LOI and lease negotiation
- Move-in coordination

**For Landlords:**
- Market pricing and vacancy analysis
- Tenant prospecting and outreach
- Offer and lease negotiation
- Occupancy and retention strategy

**Ideal for:** Businesses finding office space; office landlords managing occupancy in Greater Atlanta.

---

## 03. Investment Sales

**Subtitle:** Acquire and divest commercial assets at maximum value.

Investment decisions require a precise understanding of market fundamentals, cap rates, and structural due diligence. Trinity CRE guides buyers and sellers through every step — from sourcing and valuation to contract negotiation and closing.

**For Buyers:**
- Investment criteria definition
- On- and off-market deal sourcing
- Cap rate and return analysis
- Due diligence coordination

**For Sellers:**
- Valuation and market pricing
- Offering memorandum preparation
- Targeted buyer outreach
- Offer management and closing

**Ideal for:** Private investors, family offices, and owner-users acquiring commercial assets; owners looking to exit at optimal value.
