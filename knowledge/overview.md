## What We Do

Trinity CRE serves tenants, landlords, buyers, and sellers across three areas:
- **Industrial** — warehouses, distribution centers, manufacturing facilities, flex/R&D space
- **Office Space** — office tenant and landlord representation
- **Investment Sales** — acquisition and disposition of commercial assets

We work both sides of every deal — representing tenants finding space and landlords maximizing occupancy, buyers acquiring assets and sellers exiting at maximum value.
